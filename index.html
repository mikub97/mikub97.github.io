About Me

I am a software engineer, cognitive scientist, and educator with a deep passion for learning sciences, embodied cognition, and studying interactions between humans and digital technologies. Currently, apart from teaching, I collaborate with the Human Interactivity and Language Lab (HILL) and the Polish Academy of Sciences to explore how movement coordination and physiological clues can enhance our interactions with digital systems (e.g., collaborative robots and virtual reality).

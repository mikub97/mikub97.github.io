The city announces itself from the air: a ridge of pale buildings above the bay, the Atlantic enormous and silver-green to the east. We landed after midnight.

<!--more-->

Pelourinho in the early morning is almost quiet. The cobblestones catch the light at an angle that makes everything look painted. I had read about this place many times, but reading about somewhere is a particular kind of not-knowing.

The berimbau sound reached me before I saw the roda — drifting out of an open door somewhere on Rua Gregório de Matos. Someone practicing, not performing.

> *O berimbau toca, eu não posso ficar* — the berimbau plays, I cannot stay still.

I stood there for several minutes. This is why you have to come.
